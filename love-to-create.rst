Tonet my Love,

Let the words herein attest to all the universe and vice versa of my love for you.

How things come to be can be a wonder. With tools in tow, people sense beyond the senses to marvel at things and their wonder. Along the way, a discovery is made.
A breakthrough is realized – a eureka moment! Those who fail to fathom the immensity of these wondrous things may simply accept them as they are in amazement while 
others break from the sheer immensity and tread otherwise. They become averse to that something, taking all might and whatever is left of theirintellect and sanity
to debunk the wondrous nature of that something. Becauseof their lack of intellect and sanity, they become a lost cause. To debunk something takes a lot of 
ingenuity and courage (Schumacher, 1983)

Besides discoveries and breakthroughs, creations are a part of life. Some people just take and take. Worse, others burn
the world or want to see it so. I want to create. It has kept me sane the times I was almost a lost cause.

I am happy to have kindled this appreciation of you from that moment of discovery, whichhas eventually blossomed into 
a wonderful love story I can only imagine. Or we both can.

Thank you, my dearest, my One and Only You, Miluv and Mylabs, and My Bibi for being with me during my highs and lows - for being my everything.

I am too old by the PDI Youngblood standards and physically tooyoung to qualify for the other opinion column. I have also yet to get my space 
with a broadsheet to do these kinds of things so I just took the liberty of creatingthis space from now on to cultivate our thoughts, and initially with mine. 
I know we both love to create. May we find many more ways to create memories and tangibles. Let us create the most beautiful gifts we can unto thisuniverse 
however blip our stardust existence in it be.

We already have these pages to start these.

For you, Tonet, My Love, and everything between and beyond us to our love for each other, let us begin our foray into our love for everything under 
the sun with these pages.

Cheers to another year around the sun and let us chart an unforgettable celebration today and everyday of the being!


All Love,

Ian
